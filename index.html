The Real Cost of Guessing

Blueprints look right on paper. They feel wrong when it's too late.

Change orders are the hidden cost of every construction project — a wall that needs to move, a kitchen island two feet too wide, a bedroom that felt spacious at 14×16 on paper and feels cramped in real life. These aren't contractor mistakes. They're visualization gaps. Clients who walk their plans before ground breaks typically identify 10–12 of these issues. Fixing them in a session costs a fraction of fixing them mid-build.
